Algorithmic Pattern Catalogue

A site for exploring “algorithmic patterns”, where pattern operations are composed together and notated as an algorithm, which when followed creates results which are difficult to anticipate.

With this possibility for surprise, algorithmic patterns foreground human perception in the making process.

So far we have a forum, exploring readings around algorithmic patterns, and are building up a set of related resources.
